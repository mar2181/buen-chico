## Prior Research (From Conversation — 2026-05-11)

### Competitive Intelligence (already gathered)
- **Traini** — most dangerous competitor, AI-generated plans, English-only, small team
- **Dogo** — ~$10M ARR, subscription model, 8 languages but poor Spanish quality
- **Puppr** — ~$3M ARR, English-only, static library
- **GoodPup** — live trainer model ($30/wk), doesn't scale
- Market: bilingual gap confirmed wide open
- Hispanic pet ownership: fastest-growing segment in US
- Spanish dog training apps: effectively zero quality options

### Key Insight
> Every competitor is English-first with bolted-on translations.
> Going Spanish-first with English as L2 is the differentiation.
